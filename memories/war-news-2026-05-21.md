## Ukraine / Russia

**Current situation (as of May 21, 2026):**
- **Drone warfare deep inside Russia:** Ukraine has been striking targets ~1,000 km inside Russian territory. Reports describe Russians as "increasingly afraid" as Kyiv expands its strike reach.
- **NATO nuclear warning:** NATO has warned Putin of "devastating consequences" should Russia use nuclear weapons in or around the conflict.
- **Ceasefire collapse:** A Trump-brokered 3-day ceasefire (around May 9) collapsed almost immediately. Russia breached it within hours; Zelenskyy said Russia launched a deadly bombing spree marking the end of the truce.
- **Momentum narrative:** Multiple outlets (GZERO, The Week, NPR) assess momentum has shifted in Ukraine's favor. Russia's May 9 Victory Day parade notably featured no tanks — widely read as a sign the war is not going to plan.
- **Casualty estimates:** The NYT reported Russia has lost more than 350,000 soldiers. Approximately 2,300 North Korean soldiers sent to fight alongside Russia have also died (per BBC, May 7).
- **Foreign recruitment:** Russia is recruiting Africans and migrants — sometimes coercively — to fight in Ukraine (NYT, Al Jazeera). Xi Jinping reportedly told Trump that Putin might "regret" the invasion.
- **Belarus front:** Zelenskyy warned of a fresh threat of attack from the Belarus front (May 21).
- **Ukraine air defense:** Ukraine is deploying AI-powered interceptors and other systems in an effort to "drone-proof its skies" (BBC, May 21).
- **US warning:** US officials caution that a Ukrainian war end could heighten Russian threat to the Baltics (RFERL, May 15).
- **North Korean troops:** About 2,300 North Korean soldiers have died in the Russia-Ukraine war, new images show (BBC, May 7).
- **Economic dimension:** Russian rubles are flowing into Chinese border towns — sanctioned cars, beauty clinics — since the war began (Guardian, May 19).
- **Hungary shift:** New Hungary PM signals a shift away from the Kremlin following a massive drone attack on Ukraine (Guardian, May 13).
- **Finland:** Finland ended a drone alert amid regional fears of Ukraine war spillover (Al Jazeera, May 15).
- **EU/Ukraine:** Germany's Merz is proposing an "associate" EU membership pathway for Ukraine (Reuters, May 21).
- **UN:** The UN Security Council was told the war is "becoming deadlier by the day" (May 19).
- **Russia-China alignment:** Xi Jinping told Donald Trump that Putin might "regret" the invasion (Financial Times, May 19).

- **PLA carrier transit:** China's Fujian aircraft carrier sailed through the Taiwan Strait; Taipei tracked it closely (SCMP, Apr 20; Reuters, Apr 20). PLA warships then trained in the western Pacific after the MSDF transit.
