## Ukraine / Russia War

The Russia-Ukraine conflict remains in a grinding phase with no major territorial changes in recent weeks. Key ongoing developments:

- **Eastern Front:** Russian forces continue offensive operations around Pokrovsk and Kurakhove in Donetsk Oblast, though gains have been incremental. Ukrainian defenses have been reinforced in key sectors.
- **Drone Warfare:** Both sides have escalated drone attacks on energy infrastructure and military targets. Russia has launched multiple Shahed drones against Ukrainian cities, while Ukraine has targeted Russian oil refineries and military airfields.
- **Ceasefire Discussions:** International mediation efforts continue, though no breakthrough has been achieved. The US has resumed limited intelligence sharing with Ukraine.
- **Prisoner Exchanges:** A new exchange occurred on May 23-24, with over 100 prisoners returned to each side.
- **Military Aid:** New US defense package announced (~$300M) includes armored vehicles and artillery ammunition. European allies continue to provide equipment, though delays persist.
- **Occupied Territories:** Reports of intensified filtration operations and passportization in occupied Zaporizhzhia and Kherson oblasts.

## Middle East
