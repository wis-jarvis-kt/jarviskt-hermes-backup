## 1. Vatican Issues Major AI Encyclical: "Magnifica humanitas"

**Source:** NPR, Vatican News, National Catholic Register, The Times of Israel, Greenwich Time (May 24, 2026)

**Summary:** Pope Leo XIV issued a sweeping encyclical (formal papal document) on AI titled "Magnifica humanitas," calling it a manifesto pondering the future of humanity. The document takes direct aim at big tech companies, arguing AI must serve humanity and not concentrate power. Key themes include:
- AI must not "concentrate power" in the hands of a few
- Calls for "robust AI regulation" grounded in justice
- Condemns what he calls an "anti-human vision" in AI development
- AI governance framed as a moral and humanitarian imperative

**Significance:** This represents one of the most significant global leadership statements on AI governance from a major institution, signaling that AI ethics and regulation will be a central theme in international discourse.
