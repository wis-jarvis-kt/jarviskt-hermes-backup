## Framework Reference

**Entry Signals:**
- ✅ **Strong entry:** Both P/E < 5Y avg × 0.90 AND PEG < 1.0
- ✅ **Potential entry:** Either P/E < 5Y avg × 0.90 OR PEG < 1.0
- ❌ **No signal:** Neither condition met

**Fear & Greed Interpretation:**
- 0–25: Extreme Fear → buying opportunity
- 25–45: Fear → potentially good entry
- 45–55: Neutral
- 55–75: Greed → cautious on new buys
- 75–100: Extreme Greed → don't buy
