## 🔴 BIG RED Day — Definition

| Type | Threshold | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Index (S&P 500)** | > 1% down | Primary signal |
| **Individual Stock (Tech)** | > 2% down | Secondary confirmation |
| **Extreme Fear Drop** | > 5% cumulative over few days | Double or triple the regular amount |

**Look at S&P 500 first → then individual stock confirmation**

---

## 💰 How Much to Enter on a Red Day

| Scenario | Action | Amount |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Normal red day (<5% drop) | Regular amount | USD $500 per entry |
| Big red / Extreme fear (>5% cumulative) | **Double or triple** | USD $1,000–1,500 |
| Continue red (consecutive days) | **SKIP — have gap** between entries | Don't exhaust all bullets at once |

> *"Must have a bit of gap. Otherwise your bullet finish very fast."* — Victor

## 🟢 When to BUY (Stock)

**On a Big Red Day — YES, enter:**
- S&P 500 > 1% down → buy
- Individual tech stock > 2% down → buy
- If extreme fear (>5% drop) → double or triple normal amount

**On a Continue Red — CAUTION:**
- Multiple red days in a row → don't buy every single day
- Space your entries like ammunition
- Wait for a bounce or confirm it's truly bottomed

**On GREEN / UP day:**
- Don't buy stock directly on a green day
- You can sell CALL (covered call) on green days instead
- If you have no stock yet, wait for the next red day

---

## 🔵 When to SELL CALL (Covered Call)

**Conditions — ALL must be met:**
1. You own 100+ shares of the stock
2. It's a **GREEN day** (market/stock going up)
3. Strike price is **>10% above your average purchase price**
4. High quality company (9-10 rating)
5. IV preferably > 40%

**Why 10% above avg price:**
- Stock very unlikely to jump another 10% in same month
- Contract likely expires worthless → you keep the premium
- Think of it as "squeezing extra dividend" from shares you already own

---

## 🟡 When to SELL PUT (Cash Secured Put)

**When you have cash and want to:**
- Generate income while waiting to buy a stock you want
- "Get paid to wait" — either you collect premium or you get assigned the stock at your desired price

**Conditions:**
1. You have cash (USD $500–$1,000+ per contract)
2. It's a **RED day** (ideally big red)
3. Stock is high quality (9-10 rating)
4. Strike price at or near current market (ATM or slightly OTM)
5. Monthly expiry preferred

---

## ⛔ When to SELL (Stock) — Master KT's 4 Rules

1. **Need the money** — liquidity override
2. **Fundamentals changed** — business quality deteriorated
3. **Portfolio rebalancing** — position grew too large
4. **Found better opportunity** — higher quality company at better valuation

**No emotional selling. No stop loss needed if fundamentals are intact.**

---

## 🔄 The Wheel Strategy (Full Cycle)

```
CASH → Sell PUT (red day) → If assigned: get STOCK → Sell CALL (green day) → If exercised: get CASH back → Repeat
```

Every outcome is profitable:
- PUT expires worthless → you collected premium, kept cash
- PUT exercised → you own the stock at your price, now sell CALL
- CALL expires worthless → you collected premium, still own the stock
- CALL exercised → you sold at profit + kept premium, got cash back

---

## ⚠️ Key Victor Warnings

- **No margin / no leverage** — use cash only
- **No stop loss** — if quality is intact, hold through volatility
- **Only sell options on 9-10 quality stocks** — lower quality = you're speculating
- **Monthly contracts > annual contracts** — more compounding rounds
- **Don't rush — let the market come to you**

---

## 📊 Fear & Greed Integration

- **Fear** = good time to sell PUT (premiums higher, stocks cheaper)
- **Greed** = good time to sell CALL (stocks up, collect more premium)
- CNN Fear & Greed Index gives the ambient market mood
