### When to Buy Stock (Victor rules)
- On BIG RED day: YES — S&P500>1% down + individual tech >2% down → enter
- On continue red days: Space entries. Wait for bounce confirmation.
- On GREEN / UP day: DON'T buy stock directly. Sell CALL instead if you own shares.

- Own 100+ shares + GREEN day + strike >10% above avg purchase price + high quality (9-10) + IV >40%
- Think of it as "squeezing extra dividend" from shares you already own.

### When to Sell PUT (Victor rules)
- Have cash + RED day (ideally big red) + high quality stock (9-10) + ATM or slightly OTM strike + monthly expiry
